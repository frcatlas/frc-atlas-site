FRC Atlas
=====================================

Welcome to FRC Atlas, your comprehensive go-to resource for navigating the intricate
landscape of the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). In this website, you'll find a
treasure trove of information, from detailed tutorials and step-by-step guides to
insightful tips and strategies all in the realms of business, fabrication,
computer-aided design (CAD), electrical and more.
